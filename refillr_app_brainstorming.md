## Favorite Existing Apps - Categorize and Evaluate
### Instagram
   - **Category:** Photo & Video / Social 
   - **Mobile:** Website is view only, uses camera, mobile first experience.
   - **Story:** Allows users to share their lives in pictures and enhance their content with filters
   - **Market:** Anyone that takes pictures could enjoy this app. Ability to follow and hashtag based on interests and categories allows users with unique interests to engage with relevant content.
   - **Habit:** Users can post throughout the day many times. Features like "Stories" encourage more candid posting as well. Users can explore endless pictures in any category imaginable whenever they want. Very habit forming!
   - **Scope:** Instagram started out extremely narrow focused, just posting pics and viewing feeds. Has expanded to a somewhat larger scope including "Instagram Stories" (a la SnapChat) and messenger features. 

### Rover
   - **Category:** Pet Services / Marketplace
   - **Mobile:** Essential for location-based services, booking, and real-time communication with pet sitters
   - **Story:** Connects pet owners with trusted pet sitters and dog walkers in their area
   - **Market:** Pet owners who need reliable care for their pets when they're away or busy
   - **Habit:** Regular use for booking services, checking in on pets, and managing recurring appointments
   - **Scope:** Started with dog walking, expanded to pet sitting, boarding, and other pet services

## New App Ideas - Evaluate and Categorize

### Student Behavior Tracker
   - **Description**: Allows teachers, students and parents to stay up to date with student behavior in real time. Teachers can acknowledge positive student behavior when it happens, students can receive instant positive reinforcement and parents can be more tuned in to how their child is doing at school.
   - **Category:** Social Networking / Education
   - **Mobile:** Mobile is essential for the instant logging of student behavior. Students use the app to purchase goods from the student store and other rewards. The camera is used to share images with parents.
   - **Story:** Creates a team between student, parent and teacher. Allows good behavior to not go unnoticed and for students to get rewards and positive reinforcement from teachers, parents and peers
   - **Market:** Any school district or individual classroom teacher of any grade could utilize this app. District wide licensing and custom features could be used for monetization. 
   - **Habit:** Teachers are using this constantly throughout the school day. Students are using the app daily to check their performance and cash in on rewards.
   - **Scope:** V1 would allow teachers to access a roster of students and input behavior logs. Could actually be tested by a real teacher with a class of students. V2 would incorporate a student facing version for students to see their performance. V3 would add a way for students to earn and obtain rewards for goals met. V4 would incorporate a parent facing version.

### Refillr - Medication & Supplement Tracker
   - **Description**: Web/mobile app for tracking meds AND supplements. Helps users manage chronic health conditions by remembering dosages, timing (AM vs. PM), and tracking effects. Features include monthly refilling mode, dosage tracking, effect logging, and trusted product management.
   - **Category:** Health & Wellness / Productivity
   - **Mobile:** Mobile is essential for daily medication tracking, notifications, and quick logging throughout the day. Users need to check off medications multiple times per day (morning, afternoon, evening).
   - **Story:** Empowers users to take control of their health by making medication and supplement management simple and trackable. Helps users understand the effects of their regimen and maintain consistency.
   - **Market:** Anyone managing chronic health conditions, taking multiple medications/supplements, or wanting to track their health regimen. Particularly valuable for users with complex medication schedules.
   - **Habit:** Users check the app multiple times daily to log medications taken. Monthly refill reminders create regular engagement. Effect tracking encourages ongoing use.
   - **Scope:** V1 would include basic medication tracking with checkboxes, timing, and dosage info. V2 would add effect logging and personal rating system. V3 would incorporate refill reminders and trusted product lists. V4 would add Amazon affiliate integration and advanced analytics.

## Refillr Key Features
1. **Monthly Refilling Mode**
   - Checkboxes for each item and their details
   - Delayed notifications checking in: "Have you finished refilling <rx/supp name>?"

2. **Tracking System**
   - Track when you started/stopped medications
   - Log how much you've taken in the past
   - User can log effects they've noticed/observed
   - 5-star "personal" rating scale for effectiveness

3. **Trusted Products Management**
   - List of favorite/trusted products
   - Brand, name, dosage, count tracking
   - Link to reorder (Amazon affiliate opportunity)

4. **User Experience**
   - Login screen with "just let me in" button
   - Temporarily stores user data, but needs account to save it
   - Account setup with text authentication
